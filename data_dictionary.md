# Data Dictionary

## Participant variables
- **ID**: Participant identifier
- **SEXO**: Sex coded as 0 = Male, 1 = Female
- **EDAD**: Age in years
- **IMC**: Body mass index (kg/m²)
- **CINTURA_CM**: Waist circumference in cm

## 24-hour recall variables
- **CH_R24**: Carbohydrates estimated by 24-hour recall (g)
- **PROT_R24**: Protein estimated by 24-hour recall (g)
- **LIPIDOS_R24**: Lipids estimated by 24-hour recall (g)
- **CALO_R24**: Energy estimated by 24-hour recall (kcal)

## METAbolique variables
- **CH_METABOLIQUE**: Carbohydrates estimated by METAbolique (g)
- **PROT_METABOLIQUE**: Protein estimated by METAbolique (g)
- **LIPIDOS_METABOLIQUE**: Lipids estimated by METAbolique (g)
- **CALO_METABOLIQUE**: Energy estimated by METAbolique (kcal)

## Standardized meal reference variables
- **CH_COM_EST**: Reference carbohydrates from standardized meal (g)
- **PROT_COM_EST**: Reference protein from standardized meal (g)
- **LIPIDOS_COM_EST**: Reference lipids from standardized meal (g)
- **CALO_COM_EST**: Reference energy from standardized meal (kcal)

## Usability
- **SUS**: System Usability Scale score (0–100)
